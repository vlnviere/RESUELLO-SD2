Sebastian Sallow

Master Duelist

Sebastian Sallow is a charismatic and resourceful student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, sorted into Slytherin House. Known for his quick wit and a rebellious streak that often lands him in detention, he becomes one of the protagonist's most significant and influential companions throughout the school year. While he possesses the typical Slytherin ambition, his primary motivation is deeply personal and rooted in an unwavering sense of family loyalty.

Sebastian is an exceptionally skilled duelist who is never afraid to push the boundaries of school rules, or even the laws of magic, to achieve his specific goals. Despite his mischievous nature and a penchant for trouble, he proves to be a steadfast ally, offering his assistance and sharing his extensive knowledge of more "unconventional" spellwork. His complex personality makes him a standout character, as he constantly balances his genuine charm with a desperate, underlying intensity.

Ultimately, he represents a nuanced look at the lengths one might go to for those they love, making him a central figure in the game's broader narrative.

Defense Against the Dark Arts Class

Sebastian Sallow is widely regarded as one of the most proficient students in his year when it comes to Defense Against the Dark Arts, though his approach is famously unconventional.
He possesses an intuitive grasp of combative magic and dueling theory, often outshining his peers with his casting speed and tactical precision.
